Linux Computers

While many laptops on the market can run Linux systems, there are a handful of really great manufacturers who focus solely on systems built for Linux.

System76, based in the USA, sells computers with their own operating system called Pop!_OS. You can also choose Ubuntu LTS (Long Term Support) at the checkout.

Shop System76 »

The Raspberry Pi Foundation is a UK-based charity that works to put the power of computing and digital making into the hands of people all over the world.

Shop Raspberry Pi »

Pine Microsystems, Inc. is a US-based company which designs, manufactures and sells single-board computers, notebook computers and smartphones.

Shop Pine64 »

TUXEDO, based in Germany, sells individually built computers in many sizes. The hardware ships with Ubuntu, openSUSE or their custom TUXEDO_OS operating system.

Shop TUXEDO Computers »

Founded in Spain in 2015, Slimbook offers a wide range of Linux laptops, desktops, and all-in-one systems.

Shop Slimbook »

Ubuntu Shop makes quality notebooks with European software. Assembled in the Netherlands and software installed in Belgium. They pre-install a wide range of Linux operating systems.

Shop Ubuntu Shop »
